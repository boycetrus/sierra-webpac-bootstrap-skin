Loan Renewal Conditions

Item due dates are extended by 21 days from the date of renewal. Items on loan may be renewed up to 3 times, except in the following circumstances.

You may not renew loans online when:

  • The item you are trying to renew has been reserved by another member
  • The accrued fees on your account have exceeded the allowed limit
  • Your membership account has expired
  • We need to speak with you about another matter and staff have manually blocked your account

If your renewal is unsuccessful and you want more information please contact the library.
